Society of Peer Mentors

A Picture of the 2023 SPM Officers at the January General Meeting

I have been involved with the LSU Society of Peer Mentors since February 2022. As a peer mentor, I have enjoyed working with my peers at various volunteering opportunities, such as Geaux Engineering, a program that helps acclimate incoming freshman to succeeding in engineering.

From February 2022-February 2023 I was SPM's Points Secretary. As Points Secretary I:

  • Maintained accurate records of 131 mentors' involvement in our involvement and outreach activities.
  • Communicated with our Outreach Chairs regarding members' participation in said activities.
  • Met with the other officers and our staff advisor weekly to discuss organization updates, plan monthly meetings, etc.
